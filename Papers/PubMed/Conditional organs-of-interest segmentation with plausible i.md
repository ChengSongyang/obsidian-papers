## 摘要
BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE: Manual contouring of organs of interest (OoIs) is a major bottleneck in pancreatic magnetic resonance-guided online adaptive radiotherapy (oART). We developed C-SegDeform, a data-efficient conditional segmentation framework that used structure-guided deformation-based augmentations to simulate plausible inter-fraction anatomical variation and leveraged organ-specific conditioning as an alternative to registration-based contour propagation (Prop-ROIs) in limited-data settings. MATERIALS AND METHODS: Forty balanced 3DVane images from 12 patients were manually contoured and pre-processed, including duodenum, both kidneys, liver, large and small bowel, spinal canal, spleen, and stomach. The training dataset (26 images) was augmented by simulating plausible session images via structure-guided deformations. Data was arranged for conditional segmentation and leave-one-out cross-validation using the nnU-Net framework. Analysis included geometric (DSC, average surface distance (ASD), and 95th percentile of Hausdorff distance (HD95); against Prop-ROIs and TotalSegmentator MRI), dose (via 8 treatment plans, measuring  D 0 . 1  c m 3     and  D 50 %   discrepancies relative to prescribed dose), and clinical (Likert scale and post-auto-contour editing times by two oncology consultants) assessments. RESULTS: C-SegDeform (DSC: 0 . 88  ±  0 . 10  , ASD: 2 . 9  ±  2 . 4  mm  , HD95: 10 . 5  ±  8 . 9  mm  ) outperformed Prop-ROIs ( 0 . 70  ±  0 . 17  , 8 . 5  ±  9 . 9  mm  , 16 . 7  ±  9 . 8  mm  ) and TotalSegmentator ( 0 . 75  ±  0 . 16  , 5 . 1  ±  3 . 4  mm  , 14 . 3  ±  10 . 6  mm  ), and showed smaller relative dose deviations from ground-truth contours (  D 0 . 1  c m 3     : 2 . 2 %  ±  2 . 5 %  ,  D 50 %   : 0 . 5 %  ±  0 . 9 %  ) than Prop-ROIs ( 6 . 8 %  ±  5 . 7 %  , 3 . 3 %  ±  4 . 0 %  ). Clinicians ranked 98% of C-SegDeform contours as requiring no or minor edits (vs. 70% for Prop-ROIs), with editing times under 6 min and full OoI generation completed in under 40 s. CONCLUSIONS: C-SegDeform provided a robust alternative to contour propagation, generating accurate contours rapidly with minimal edits, thereby reducing on-couch time and streamlining the workflow.

## 深度总结

**1. 研究背景与大问题**
胰腺磁共振引导在线自适应放疗（oART）中，手动勾画感兴趣器官（OoI）是主要瓶颈。在数据有限的情况下，基于配准的轮廓传播（Prop-ROIs）方法精度不足。如何在小样本条件下实现准确的自动器官分割是关键挑战。

**2. 具体研究问题**
（1）如何在有限数据下实现胰腺MR引导放疗的自动器官分割？（2）结构引导的变形增强能否模拟合理的分次间解剖变异？（3）分割精度和剂量学影响如何？

**3. 研究方法**
从12例患者中获取40幅平衡3DVane图像，手动勾画十二指肠、双肾、肝脏、大小肠、脊髓管、脾脏和胃。开发C-SegDeform框架：使用结构引导变形增强模拟合理的分次间解剖变异，结合器官特异性条件分割。基于nnU-Net框架进行留一交叉验证。评估几何指标（DSC、ASD、HD95）、剂量学指标（D0.1cm3、D50%偏差）和临床评估（Likert量表和编辑时间）。

**4. 主要发现**
C-SegDeform（DSC: 0.88±0.10, ASD: 2.9±2.4mm, HD95: 10.5±8.9mm）显著优于Prop-ROIs（0.70±0.17, 8.5±9.9mm, 16.7±9.8mm）和TotalSegmentator（0.75±0.16, 5.1±3.4mm, 14.3±10.6mm）。剂量偏差更小（D0.1cm3: 2.2%±2.5% vs 6.8%±5.7%）。临床医师评估98%的C-SegDeform轮廓无需或仅需少量编辑（vs Prop-ROIs的70%），编辑时间<6分钟。

**5. 结论与意义**
C-SegDeform在数据有限条件下显著提升了胰腺MR引导放疗的器官分割精度，具有临床实用价值。该方法通过模拟合理的分次间变异有效增强了数据，减少了对手动勾画的依赖。

**6. 局限性与展望**
样本量较小（12例患者），需在更大队列中验证。未来可扩展到其他解剖部位和放疗场景。
